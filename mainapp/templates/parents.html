Safety All guides are verified students at an accredited US university or college. We accept only the best of college students into our program as guides. Our guides must maintain a cumulative GPA of 2.5 or higher to participate. For overnight and full weekend visits, visitors reside with guides of their own gender. For further ease of mind, all guides also undergo extensive background checks administered by Checkr, Inc.
Learn more about Checkr! Communication At University Visits, we also understand that your ability to remain in constant contact with your child is a must. We supply all visitors with their guide’s phone number and email address, along with an ability to reach them through our online messaging platform. In the event that a visitor misplaces or loses access to their mobile device and a parent can no longer reach their child directly, parents can get in touch with their child’s guide using the supplied contact information.

Tidy up a little

For overnight & full weekend visits, make sure your off-campus housing unit is cleaned up a bit. No need for luxury. Just make sure it’s not a mess!
Since your visitors stay overnight with you in your off-campus housing for overnight & full weekend visits, you need to supply your visitors with makeshift sleeping accommodations. These are college sleeping accommodations and are meant to mirror the informal and casual atmosphere of college life. So simply couches, sleeping bags, and makeshift beds are perfectly fine! Get some pillows and blankets too! You also need essentials for your overnight visitors – soap, shampoo, towels, toilet paper. Always keep these handy for your visitors!
